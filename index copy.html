About Us

Bildung mit Wirkung e.V. - A bridge between Germany and Indonesia

Bildung mit Wirkung e.V. is a non-profit organization officially registered in Munich, Germany. We are dedicated to bringing equitable education and dignified care to communities in remote areas of Indonesia through sustainable programs and cross-cultural collaboration.

With experience from Germany's structured social system and the spirit of Indonesian community, we build bridges of solidarity to provide access, independence, and dignity.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all donations channeled transparently?
Yes. We apply the principle of transparency: concise reports for each campaign are available and full annual reports can be downloaded on the Transparency page.
How can I become a volunteer?
Fill out the contact form, mentioning your interests and availability. Our team will contact you for the selection process and a brief orientation.
Is this organization officially registered in Germany?
Yes — Bildung mit Wirkung e.V. is a registered organization (registered e.V.) in Munich, Germany.
Can I donate from outside Germany?
Yes. We accept international donations. Information on donation methods and bank accounts is available on the Donation / Contact page.
