What We Do

Our Programs

Our mission focuses on two main pillars: providing equitable education access for children in remote areas, and ensuring dignified care for the elderly.

Education Access & Aid

Providing proper learning facilities in remote villages. Challenges include: damaged school buildings, teacher shortages, and minimal electricity & internet. We distribute books, uniforms, and learning materials.

See details

Elderly Care & Dignity

This program targets elderly individuals who mostly depend on their families. We offer basic health check-ups, food aid, and social support to ensure their dignity is maintained.

See details

Community Capacity Building

We work independently, free from political or religious ties, alongside local partners (Lentera Aksara Negeri Foundation) to train local volunteers and build sustainable programs.

See details

Cross-Cultural Exchanges

Facilitating knowledge exchange between our partners in Germany and communities in Indonesia to find innovative, sustainable local solutions, bridging both cultures.

See details

Disaster Relief

Helping families affected by floods, fires, earthquakes, and other natural disasters with emergency aid such as food, shelter, and medical supplies.
